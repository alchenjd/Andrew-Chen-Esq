Practice Area 02
Immigration
Law
Navigating the U.S. immigration system demands both precision and compassion. From skilled worker visas that support business growth to family reunification and removal defense, this practice provides clear-eyed guidance through every stage of the immigration journey — before USCIS, the immigration courts, and beyond.
  • Employment-based visas: H-1B, L-1, O-1, TN, and EB preference categories
  • Family-based petitions and adjustment of status
  • Naturalization and citizenship applications
  • DACA renewals and special immigrant juvenile status
  • Consular processing and visa interview preparation
  • Removal defense and immigration court representation
  • Corporate immigration compliance and I-9 audits
